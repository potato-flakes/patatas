Campuses


The grounds and buildings of DHVSU

Bacolor Campus

Main campus of Don Honorio Ventura State University

Mexico Campus

The DHVSU – Mexico Campus (DMC) was initially established as an extension campus during the administration of former University President Ernesto T. Nicdao, at the time when the lahar calamity was still a threat in Bacolor.

Porac Campus

DHVTSU Porac campus was established and started to offer ladderized courses on Hotel and Restaurant Management, Information, Technical Teacher Education and Industrial technology.

Sto Tomas Campus

DHVSU - Sto. Tomas Campus is located at: San Nicolas Road, 2020 Santo Tomas, Pampanga.

Lubao Campus

September 27, 1996 saw the beginning of the DHVSU Lubao Campus when the Governing Board of the Don Honorio Ventura State University, then still named the Don Honorio Ventura College of Arts and Trades

Candaba Campus

DHVSU Candaba Campus was established as an extension campus on September 2, 2019 under the administration of Honorable Governor Dennis Pineda and others.

Apalit Campus

Message of the President


DHVSU President

It is my pleasure to welcome you to the DHVSU website.

A multitude of facts about the university can be found in this website. You can take a glimpse at our vision and mission, our responsive educational programs, innovative research, achievements and milestones, and the administrators, faculty, personnel, students, alumni and friends who constitute the proud DHVSU community...

DHVSU has achieved a great deal since it was founded 1861. As the oldest vocational school not only in the country but in the whole Far East, the university constantly strives to develop an educated, empowered and economically – productive Filipino citizenry through innovative and responsive programs embracing theory and practice.

Our main focus is on the creation of learner-centered community that encourage the intellectual development and transformation of students into leaders, critical thinkers and lifelong learners.

In the years ahead, the university is foreseen to nurture individuals who possess the foundations for learning throughout life, the competence to engage in gainful work and be productive, and the values and abilities to coexist in prolific harmony with local and global communities.

We yearn to realize this aspiration through the continuous upgrading of instruction; enhancement of access to quality education; intensification of knowledge-generation; improvement of organizational efficiency; enrichment of organizational governance; provision of modern facilities and state-of-the-art equipment; promotion of social responsibility through extension, manpower and skills development training and community outreach services; and the establishment of consortia and other forms of linkages with local and foreign institutions.

I hope that you will find delight in your visit to our website and discover the information you search for. Thank you and God Bless!

DR. ENRIQUE G. BAKING
University President
